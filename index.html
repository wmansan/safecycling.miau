About Us

Our advocacy promotes safer urban cycling by addressing key challenges such as unsafe roads, poorly marked crosswalks, and dangerous intersections. Through dedicated bike lanes, improved infrastructure, and community engagement, we aim to create sustainable and cyclist-friendly cities that benefit everyone.

Projects

  • Organizing workshops on cycling safety and infrastructure
  • Engaging city planners for better cycling infrastructure
  • Partnering with local schools and businesses to promote cycling
